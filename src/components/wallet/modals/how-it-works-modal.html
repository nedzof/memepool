How It Works

1

Connect Your Wallet

Choose from our supported wallets or create a new one to get started. Your wallet is your gateway to the BSV ecosystem.

2

Fund Your Wallet

Add BSV to your wallet through supported exchanges or by receiving from another wallet. You'll need BSV to participate in competitions.

3

Start Creating

Choose a block to compete in, create your meme, and submit it. Your creation will be permanently stored on the BSV blockchain.

4

Earn Rewards

Top submissions earn BSV rewards. The more engagement your meme gets, the more you earn. Rewards are automatically sent to your wallet.
